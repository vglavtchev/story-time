The Sweetest Roll

Alex is seven. Maya is eleven. They both love skateboarding and eating ice cream.

Turn the page →

Meet Alex and Maya

Maya is eleven and smooth on every curve. Alex is seven and full of brave wobbles.

Tricks first, scoops second

"Land the roll," Maya says, "and we celebrate with double scoops." Alex grins so hard his helmet tilts.

The wobble

Alex leans. The board argues. Maya reaches out with one hand and says, "Again. You are closer than you think."

The sweetest roll

Alex glides through the wobble like it was only weather. Maya cheers. Even the pavement seems to clap.

Ice cream, at last

Maya picks strawberry-swirl bravery. Alex picks blue moon sky. Sticky fingers, loud laughter, and a story worth retelling.
